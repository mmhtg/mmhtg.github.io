Men's Mental Health Talking Group in Cornwall, UK

What

Men's mental health talking group

Informal round table group sessions guided by trained male facilitators. Confidential & non-judgemental.

No pressure to talk, you decide your involvement. No booking or referral, just turn up.

Free to attend. Tea, coffee, water & biscuits provided.

When

Fridays, 7 – 9pm

Weekly meetings in autumn & winter. Fortnightly meetings in spring & summer with additional outdoor events.

Times

  • Early doors: 6:30pm
  • 1st half: 7pm – 7:55pm
  • Break: 7:55pm – 8:05pm
  • 2nd half: 8:05pm – 9pm
  • Extra time & close: 9pm

Who

Meeting led by

Pete Cooper is Otterham's meeting facilitator. He's run men's mental health talking group meetings across Cornwall since spring 2023.

He's a Mental Health First Aider, is trained in ASIST suicide intervention, has extended DBS certification, and completed Peer Support Worker training to Supervisor level.

Your First Meeting

[Work in progress.]

You are welcome to attend a full (2 hour) or half (1 hour) meeting. You can talk (share) with the rest of the group, but you are never under any pressure. It is common for some new attendees to take some time before sharing, while other attendees talk in their first session. You decide the level of your group participation.

Please arrive at the venue before 7pm or during the halftime break, if you can. This helps us to welcome you with minimal disruption to other group members who are sharing during the meeting. Doors open around 6:30pm for setup, and there is a 10-minute comfort break around 8pm.
