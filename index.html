Men's Mental Health Talking Group in Cornwall, UK

What

Men's mental health talking support group

We run informal, round table support group talking sessions guided by trained male facilitators. We are a supportive group of men sharing real-life experience, advice & support in a safe location.

Confidential & non-judgemental. Free to attend. Never any pressure to talk. If you choose to talk, we will listen & you will be heard.

You decide your involvement. No booking or referral, just turn up. Tea, coffee, water & biscuits provided.

When

Fortnightly indoor meetings in spring & summer with group-organised outdoor events on alternate weeks.

Who

Group meetings led by…

He's a Mental Health First Aider, trained in ASIST suicide intervention, has enhanced DBS certification, and has completed Peer Support Worker training to Supervisor level.

Your First Meeting

[Work in progress.]

You are welcome to join a full (2 hour) meeting, or either half (1 hour) if that feels more appropriate.

You can talk (share) with the the group, or you can listen. You are never pressured to talk. It is common for new attendees to take time before sharing, while others talk openly in their first meeting. You choose your route in.

Please arrive at the venue before 7pm or during the halftime break from 7:55pm – 8:05pm, if you can. This helps us welcome you properly with minimal disruption to other group members who are sharing during the talking meeting.

Support & Donations

[Work in progress.]

Meetings are free to join & attend. There is no entrance fee or subscription to pay. Meetings are funded by donations from our supporters. Attendees are never asked for donations, but a discreet donation collection pot is located in the kitchen away from the meeting should an attendee wish to donate. Donations are kept locally and used towards funding the running costs of the meeting, including venue hire, refreshments, liability insurance etc.

Broadcast Channels

Channels are used for one-way info message broadcasts. Messages include a reminder for the meeting a few days beforehand, and important time-sensitive messages affecting the meeting.

Your phone number is not shown or shared with anyone. Nobody else will know you are following. You can unfollow / follow at any time.
