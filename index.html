Men's talking group

When

Fridays, 7–9pm.
Venue open from 6:30pm. First half 7pm to 7:55pm, second half 8:05pm to 9pm.

Schedule

Clock changes in March (spring & summer) & October (autumn & winter) guide our schedule:

  • Autumn & winter: weekly meetings.
  • Spring & summer: fortnightly meetings.

We organise regular outdoor meetups during the spring & summer schedule.

Meeting Information

Round table meeting. Tea, coffee, water & biscuits provided.
